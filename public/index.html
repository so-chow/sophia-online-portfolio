about me

I was born in Canada, raised by my Chinese parents, and I traveled the world before I was 10. My father's work took us to many rich cultures from Caracas,Venezuela, Buenos Aires, Argentina, and Houston, Texas. I moved to Austin, Texas in 2004 to study at The University of Texas at Austin to be an educator. Being a teacher for 10 years has given me essential skills in effective communication, endless patience, organization, creative problem solving skills, and standing up for people and families that need the most support.

As a lover of learning, creating, and building genuine connections, I want to merge two of my passions of web development and teaching to make a positive mark in this world. Using my skills in these areas, I want to make life the slightest bit easier for the people around me.
